Night on Earth

A poster focused on metaphor based on the Paris scene from Jim Jarmusch's film Night on Earth. A negative space thumbprint type created from my own thumbprint emphasises the blind characters reliance on touch, while the silver reflects a vague image, reducing the viewer's own vision. The marks and scratches in the black give the appearance that the poster has been handled, again placing an emphasis on touch.
